Cologne, Germany
Why..

I want to visit Cologne, Germany because of its rich history, stunning architecture, and deep religious significance. My mother, being very religious, has always wanted to visit, and I would love to take her there one day. The city is home to magnificent churches, especially the famous Cologne Cathedral, a breathtaking Gothic masterpiece that stands as one of the most iconic landmarks in Europe.

Aside from its religious and historical charm, Cologne holds a special place in my heart because it was a filming location for one of my favorite K-dramas. Seeing the beautiful streets and landmarks in person after watching them on screen would make the experience even more exciting. Visiting Cologne would be a meaningful journey, both for my mother and for my own love of history, culture, and film.

Things I wanted to do:
  • Visit the Cologne Cathedral (Kölner Dom) – Admire the stunning Gothic architecture of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Explore the Old Town (Altstadt) – Wander through charming streets with colorful buildings and historic landmarks.
  • Walk along the Hohenzollern Bridge – See thousands of love locks attached to the bridge and enjoy scenic views of the Rhine River.
  • Visit the Ludwig Museum – Discover modern art masterpieces, including works by Picasso and Warhol.
  • Try authentic Kölsch beer – Experience Cologne’s famous local beer in a traditional brewery.
  • Explore the Chocolate Museum (Schokoladenmuseum) – Learn about the history of chocolate and enjoy delicious samples.
  • Take a Rhine River cruise – Enjoy breathtaking views of Cologne’s skyline from the water.
  • Visit the Cologne Zoo – See a variety of animals in one of Germany’s oldest zoos.
  • Shop at Schildergasse – Explore one of the busiest shopping streets in Germany for fashion and souvenirs.
  • Attend the Cologne Carnival – Experience one of Europe’s biggest and most vibrant street festivals if visiting in February.
Other things I wanted to do:
  • Visit the Roman-Germanic Museum – Explore ancient Roman artifacts and Cologne’s rich history.
  • Relax at the Claudius Therme – Enjoy a thermal spa experience with saunas and hot spring pools.
  • Take a scenic walk through Rheinpark – Stroll through a beautiful park with great views of the Rhine River.
  • Explore the Belgian Quarter – Discover trendy cafés, boutiques, and vibrant street art.
  • Visit the Farina Fragrance Museum – Learn about the origins of Eau de Cologne and its history.
