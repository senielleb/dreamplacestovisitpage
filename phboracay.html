Boracay, Aklan
Why..
I want to visit Boracay because of its stunning white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant island life. Growing up by the beach, I’ve always had a deep love for the ocean, and Boracay’s paradise-like beauty feels like a dream destination. My mother shares the same excitement about visiting, and we both want to experience everything the island has to offer. I’ve seen countless breathtaking photos online, and each one makes me even more fascinated by its beauty. From the picturesque sunsets to the exciting activities and lively atmosphere, Boracay looks like the perfect place to have fun, unwind, and see what all the hype is about!
Things I wanted to:
  • Witness a breathtaking sunset – I can't wait to see the sky painted in hues of orange, pink, and purple while relaxing on the shore.
  • Try all the exciting water activities – From kayaking to paragliding, I want to experience the thrill of Boracay’s famous water adventures.
  • Relax on White Beach – Walk along the powdery white sand and enjoy the crystal-clear waters.
  • Taste Boracay’s local delicacies – Try fresh seafood, the famous Chori Burger, and a refreshing glass of Jonah’s fruit shake.
  • Go island hopping – Visit nearby islands like Puka Beach, Crystal Cove, and Crocodile Island for more stunning views.
  • Experience the vibrant nightlife – Enjoy fire-dancing performances, live music, and beach parties under the stars.
  • Snorkeling and diving – Explore Boracay’s colorful marine life and coral reefs.
  • Paddleboarding – Glide across the calm waters while enjoying the tropical scenery.
  • Get a relaxing beachfront massage – Unwind with a soothing massage while listening to the sound of the waves.
  • Take Instagram-worthy photos – Capture every stunning view, from the shoreline to the fun adventures.
Other things I wanted to:
  • Braid my hair – Get a stylish beach braid for the full island experience.
  • Discover more local foods – Try delicacies like calamansi muffins, seafood platters, and the famous coconut ice cream.
  • Stay at a nice hotel – Relax in a beachfront resort with stunning views and great
  • Explore D’Mall – Stroll around Boracay’s shopping and dining hub for souvenirs
  • Go on a sunset cruise – Sail on a traditional paraw boat while watching the sun set over the horizon.
