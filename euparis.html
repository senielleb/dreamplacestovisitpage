Paris, France
Why..

I want to visit Paris, France because of its timeless elegance, rich history, and artistic treasures. While the Eiffel Tower is undoubtedly the most hyped landmark, my fascination with Paris goes beyond that. I've always been intrigued by the city's deep connection to art, especially the Louvre Museum, which houses some of the most famous paintings in the world. Seeing masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo in person would be a dream come true.

I've also heard that French people can be quite intimidating, which only adds to my curiosity about their culture and way of life. Paris is also known for its exquisite pastries, and I can't wait to indulge in authentic croissants, macarons, and other delicious treats. As the "City of Love," it has a romantic charm that makes me hope I can visit one day with someone special. Hopefully, when that time comes, I’ll get to experience Paris in all its beauty, culture, and charm.

Things I wanted to do:
  • Visit the Louvre Museum – Admire world-famous artworks like the Mona Lisa and Venus de Milo.
  • See the Eiffel Tower at night – Experience the magical sparkle of the tower as it lights up every hour.
  • Put a love lock on Pont des Arts – Symbolize love by attaching a lock to this famous bridge.
  • Discover charming pastry cafés – Indulge in croissants, macarons, éclairs, and other French delights.
  • Eat authentic French cuisine – Try escargot, coq au vin, ratatouille, and French onion soup.
  • Explore Montmartre – Wander through the artistic streets and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  • Walk along the Champs-Élysées – Shop at luxury boutiques and enjoy the lively Parisian atmosphere.
  • Visit the Palace of Versailles – Marvel at the grand halls, gardens, and historic architecture.
  • Cruise along the Seine River – Enjoy a scenic boat tour with breathtaking views of Paris landmarks.
  • Explore the Catacombs of Paris – Discover the eerie underground tunnels filled with historical bones.
Other things I wanted to do:
  • Experience Parisian nightlife – Visit lively bars, jazz clubs, and rooftop lounges with stunning city views.
  • Watch a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge – Enjoy an iconic Parisian performance filled with music and dance.
  • Stroll along the Seine River – Take a romantic nighttime walk with the city lights reflecting on the water.
  • Enjoy a late-night café experience – Sip wine or coffee at a charming Parisian café while people-watching.
  • Visit a rooftop bar – Admire breathtaking views of Paris while enjoying cocktails at spots like Le Perchoir or Terrass Hotel.
