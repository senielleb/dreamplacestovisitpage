Coron, Palawan
Why..
I want to visit Coron, Palawan because of its breathtaking natural beauty, crystal-clear lakes, and stunning limestone cliffs. Known for its world-class diving spots, hidden lagoons, and pristine beaches, Coron is a tropical paradise that feels like a dream destination. More than just a personal desire, this trip holds a deeper meaning for me—I promised my mother that once I have a stable job, I would take her there and let her stay for as long as she wants. I know that traveling to Coron can be quite expensive, but I believe the experience will be worth every penny. Seeing my mother happy and experiencing the beauty of Coron together will make it all worthwhile.
Things I wanted to do:
  • Take stunning Instagram-worthy photos – Capture Coron’s breathtaking landscapes, crystal-clear waters, and scenic views.
  • Deep dive to explore marine life – Discover vibrant coral reefs and historic World War II shipwrecks underwater.
  • Island-hop to stunning destinations – Visit Malcapuya Island, Banana Island, and Bulog Dos Island for white-sand beaches and turquoise waters.
  • Swim in Kayangan Lake – Experience one of the cleanest and most picturesque lakes in the Philippines.
  • Visit Barracuda Lake – Explore its unique mix of freshwater and saltwater with dramatic limestone cliffs.
  • Relax in Maquinit Hot Springs – Soak in its natural saltwater hot springs surrounded by mangroves.
  • Hike up Mt. Tapyas – Climb to the summit for a stunning panoramic view of Coron, especially at sunset.
  • Snorkel at Twin Lagoon – Swim through dramatic limestone formations and crystal-clear waters.
  • Rent a kayak – Paddle through peaceful lagoons and coastal waters for a serene island experience.
Other things I wanted to do:
  • Indulge in fresh seafood – Savor Coron’s best seafood dishes, from grilled fish to fresh crabs and shrimp.
  • Shop for souvenirs – Bring home locally made crafts, pearls, and other unique Coron souvenirs.
  • Visit the Coron Public Market – Explore the bustling market and experience local flavors and fresh produce.
  • Explore Calauit Safari Park – See exotic wildlife, including giraffes and zebras, in Coron’s famous wildlife sanctuary.
  • Enjoy a relaxing beachside massage – Unwind with a traditional Filipino hilot massage by the shore.
