Rome, Italy
Why..

I want to visit Rome, Italy because of its rich history, breathtaking art, and world-famous cuisine. As a city that has preserved centuries of artistic and architectural masterpieces, Rome feels like an open-air museum. From intricate sculptures to stunning paintings, the city's deep connection to art fascinates me, and I would love to see iconic landmarks like the Vatican Museums, the Sistine Chapel, and the Colosseum in person.

Aside from its art and history, Rome is also a food lover’s paradise. As the birthplace of pasta and pizza, the city offers some of the most authentic and delicious Italian dishes. I can't wait to indulge in a classic Roman pasta like Carbonara or Cacio e Pepe and try a true Italian-style pizza. Rome’s culture, art, and food make it a dream destination, and I look forward to experiencing everything that makes this city so legendary.

Things I wanted to do:
  • Eat authentic Italian pizza and pasta – Savor classic dishes like Carbonara, Cacio e Pepe, and Margherita pizza.
  • Visit the Colosseum – Explore one of the most iconic ancient Roman landmarks.
  • Throw a coin into the Trevi Fountain – Make a wish and admire the stunning Baroque masterpiece.
  • Explore the Vatican City – Visit St. Peter’s Basilica, the Vatican Museums, and the Sistine Chapel.
  • Walk through the Roman Forum – Discover the ruins of ancient Rome’s political and social hub.
  • Climb the Spanish Steps – Enjoy the lively atmosphere and beautiful views of Piazza di Spagna.
  • Visit the Pantheon – Marvel at the ancient temple’s impressive dome and historical significance.
  • Explore Trastevere – Wander through charming streets filled with cafés, street art, and local markets.
  • Try authentic Italian gelato – Taste rich, creamy gelato from renowned gelaterias like Giolitti or Fatamorgana.
  • Take a sunset stroll along the Tiber River – Enjoy the picturesque views of Rome’s bridges and historic buildings.
Other things I wanted to do:
  • Visit the Castel Sant’Angelo – Explore the historic fortress and enjoy panoramic views of Rome.
  • Shop at Campo de' Fiori Market – Experience a lively market with fresh produce, souvenirs, and local delicacies.
  • Discover the Catacombs of Rome – Walk through underground tunnels filled with ancient Christian tombs.
  • Watch the sunset from Gianicolo Hill – Enjoy one of the best scenic views of Rome.
  • Take a food tour – Indulge in Rome’s culinary delights, including espresso, tiramisu, and artisanal cheeses.
